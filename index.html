WHO I AM

I am a student of Adamas University.

There’s a lot of hype around coding, but still we find in India people are struggling to get a job in this field. This is because of the lack of applications based learning over tutorial loop that we all face. We want to change this by developing apps which have real world benefits and hands on workshops. We learn and grow together which will help us and the world in better project management and skill development.
